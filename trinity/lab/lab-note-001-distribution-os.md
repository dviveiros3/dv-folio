## Thesis
Distribution is the primary constraint for technical founders and solopreneurs. It is often treated as a "content volume" problem, but it is actually a "systems architecture" problem. The Trinity Distribution OS is designed to turn raw expertise into a repeatable market presence using five compounding loops.

## The Five-Loop System
We have codified our distribution strategy into five distinct loops. Each loop is designed to provide leverage for the next, compressing the time between "insight" and "conversion."

1.  **POV (Point of View) Loop:** Scale your taste. Instead of generic advice, we publish a clear market thesis until the market associates the founder with a specific problem/transformation.
2.  **Proof Loop:** Show the work. We move from "trust me" to "inspect this" by building and publishing artifacts, experiments, and lessons.
3.  **Conversation Loop:** Listen for signal. We turn audience attention into direct, qualified conversations with buyers.
4.  **Offer Loop:** Productize the pain. We convert repeated buyer objections and needs into productized audits, kits, and workflows.
5.  **Conversion Loop:** Close the circuit. We use market feedback to refine the engine and repeat the cycle.
