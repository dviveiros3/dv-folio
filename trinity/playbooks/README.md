# Trinity Playbooks

Step-by-step guides for solving real business problems with AI. Sellable as digital products, course materials, or consulting frameworks.

## Playbook Criteria
- [ ] Solves a specific, high-value business problem
- [ ] Includes templates, checklists, and reusable frameworks
- [ ] Tested with at least 2 real-world scenarios
- [ ] Includes ROI calculator or success metrics
- [ ] Ready to be automated into a tool or SaaS
