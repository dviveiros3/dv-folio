# Templates

## 1. Customer Interview Script
- What tools do you use for cold outreach?
- How many hours per week do you spend on it?
- What is your biggest frustration?
- What would save you the most time?
- What would you pay for the right solution?
- Can you think of 3 other people with the same pain?

## 2. User Story Template
As a [type of user], I want [goal], so that [benefit].
Acceptance Criteria:
- Given [context], when [action], then [outcome]

## 3. Beta Feedback Form
- What were your first impressions?
- What did you accomplish today?
- What frustrated you?
- What did you not use?
- What feature is missing?
- How likely are you to pay? ($ / $$$ / $$$$$)

## 4. Launch Checklist
- Product ready?
- Landing page done?
- Payment processing tested?
- Analytics tracking active?
- Support ready?
- Email campaign ready?
- Social posts scheduled?
- Influencers notified?

## 5. Analytics Dashboard Template
Track weekly:
- Signups
- Activations (completed first sequence)
- Paid conversions
- Churn (canceled subscriptions)
- NPS score
- Feature usage (top 5 features)
