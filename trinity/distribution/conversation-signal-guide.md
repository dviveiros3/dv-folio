# Trinity Distribution: Conversation & Signal Capture Guide (Week 1)

## Purpose
This guide is the operational layer for the **Growth Distribution Sprint (Week 1)**. Publishing content only matters if it creates qualified signal, uncovers buyer objections, and helps us refine the **AI Growth Audit** offer.

Use this guide to handle replies, comments, and DMs from the Week 1 publishing packet and turn them into market intelligence.

---

## 1. Reply Triage Categories
Not all engagement is equal. Categorize replies before responding to prioritize signal over noise.

| Category | Description | Goal |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Curious Founder** | Asks "how-to" or "how does this apply to me?" | Educate on the Distribution OS framework. |
| **Skeptical Operator** | Questions the feasibility or points out "AI slop" risks. | Acknowledge validity; show the human-in-the-loop/proof discipline. |
| **Potential Buyer** | Mentions a specific bottleneck (e.g., "I have no proof artifacts"). | Move to DM; qualify for Growth Audit. |
| **Peer / Validator** | High-fives the thesis or adds their own expertise. | Build reputation; reinforce the POV. |
| **Noise** | Generic "Great post!" or bot-like engagement. | Minimum engagement (like/ignore). |

## 3. Qualification Rubric: When to suggest the Growth Audit
Suggest the **AI Growth Audit** ONLY if the conversation hits 2+ of these markers:

1.  **Identity:** They are a founder, SME, or growth operator.
2.  **Pain:** They explicitly mention "random acts of content" or "lack of repeatable distribution."
3.  **Action:** they have an existing product, offer, or deep expertise (even if unscaled).
4.  **Intent:** They ask how Daniel's specific "Distribution OS" works for their case.

**Soft-Close Script:**
> "Based on what you're saying about [specific pain], it sounds like your [specific loop] is the current leak. I'm running a few AI Growth Audits this month to map this out for founders. It’s a 1-week diagnostic that gives you a 90-day roadmap. Would it be useful to see the sample deliverable?"

## 5. Proof Discipline Notes (What NOT to claim)
To keep Trinity defensible, avoid these claims in replies or DMs:

*   **NO:** "This system will guarantee you 10 leads a week."
*   **NO:** "I've used this to generate $X million for other clients." (Refer to `proof-inventory.md` for what is allowed).
*   **NO:** "The AI does all the work for you."
*   **Instead Use:** "Designed to surface signal," "Framework for scaling judgment," "Diagnostic roadmap."
