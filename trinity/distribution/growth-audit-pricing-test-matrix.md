# AI Growth Audit: Paid Beta Pricing Test Matrix

## Mission
This matrix defines the pricing hypotheses for the AI Growth Audit paid beta. It operationalizes the "Offer Intelligence Loop" by treating price as a signal of value and a filter for buyer quality, rather than a fixed number. We use these hypotheses to find the point where price reflects the value of the diagnostic without creating unnecessary friction for the initial signal capture.

## 1. Pricing Hypotheses

| Hypothesis | Price Point (Test Range) | Rationale | Buyer Signal | Objections to Listen For |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **The Wedge** | $500 - $950 | Minimize friction for rapid signal capture. Optimized for high-volume intake testing and hardening the diagnostic workflows. | "This is a low-risk experiment to see if the Trinity system can find my leaks." | "Is this just a generic AI-generated report?" / "Is it too cheap to be deep?" |
| **The Operator** | $1,500 - $2,500 | Anchored against the opportunity cost of founder/operator time. Positions the audit as an efficiency gain for manual distribution. | "This is an investment in operational clarity and a blueprint for my team." | "I can probably do this myself with ChatGPT." / "Can you show me the direct ROI?" |
| **The Architect** | $3,500 - $5,000 | Positioned as high-conviction architectural design. Implies deep diagnostic depth and a long-term strategic implementation roadmap. | "This is the master blueprint for my distribution engine for the next 12 months." | "Why does a 1-week diagnostic cost $5k?" / "Does this include the build?" |

## 2. Price Adjustment Triggers (Offer Intelligence Loop)

| Action | Triggers (Evidence-Based) |
| --- | --- |
| **Raise Price** | 3 consecutive "Yes" decisions with zero price friction; Buyer suggests the value exceeds the cost; "Observed" quality of audit output is consistently high. |
| **Hold Price** | 50%+ conversion from qualified discovery calls (Score 4+) to paid audit; Objections are focused on "Timing" or "Founder Inputs" rather than "Value." |
| **Reduce Price** | 3 consecutive "No" decisions specifically citing "Price" despite high qualification scores; Friction indicates the "Wedge" is too thick for the current "Verified" proof. |

## 3. Evidence Required for Public Offer Update
Before updating the price placeholders in the [Public Offer Sheet](../catalog/growth-kit/growth-audit-paid-beta-offer-sheet.md), the following evidence is required:

1.  **Verified Signal:** Minimum of 2 payments received at the test price point.
2.  **Delivered Value:** Buyer confirmation that the [90-Day Roadmap](../catalog/growth-kit/growth-audit.md) is actionable and specific.
3.  **Segment Alignment:** Confirmation that the price point is attracting the "Ideal Buyer" (Technical Founders/SMEs) rather than "Magic Button" seekers.

## 4. Beta Conversation Tracker: Price Reaction Fields
*To maintain the Intelligence Loop, these fields must be logged in the [Beta Conversation Tracker](./growth-audit-beta-conversation-tracker.md) after every discovery call.*

- **Price Hypothesis Quoted:** (Wedge / Operator / Architect + Exact $)
- **Buyer Reaction (Exact Language):** Record the first phrase they say after the price is mentioned.
- **Price Friction Score (1-5):** (1: "That's it?" -> 5: "Total deal-breaker")
- **Competitor/Alternative Anchor:** Did they compare the price to a consultant, a course, or a software tool?

## 5. Initial Beta Price Recommendation
**Recommended Range:** $950 - $1,500
**Rationale:** This range sits between "The Wedge" and "The Operator." It is high enough to require founder commitment and filter for serious "Subject Matter Experts," but low enough to reflect the "Beta" status of the lab's diagnostic tools. It allows for a high-status "Paid Beta" framing that respects the founder's time and expertise.
