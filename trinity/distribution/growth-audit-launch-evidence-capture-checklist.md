# Growth Audit: Launch Evidence Capture Checklist

## Purpose
This checklist operationalizes the "Offer Intelligence Loop" for the Trinity Growth Audit paid beta. It ensures that every launch activity—from publishing a POV post to a discovery call—is captured as structured evidence rather than "vibes." This allows us to improve the offer weekly based on documented market signal rather than speculation.

---

## 1. Pre-Publishing: Asset Verification
*Complete these checks before any post, artifact, or lab note goes live.*

- [ ] **Artifact Status:** Is the artifact being shared listed as "Active" in the [Proof Artifact Index](./growth-audit-proof-artifact-index.md)?
- [ ] **Confidence Labeling:** Is the artifact clearly labeled (Verified, Observed, Simulated, etc.)?
- [ ] **Simulated Disclaimer:** If sharing a sample (e.g., Audit Sample), is the "Simulated" or "Sample" label visible in the screenshot?
- [ ] **Intended Loop:** Which Distribution OS loop (POV, Proof, Conversations, Offer, Conversion) is this post designed to trigger?
- [ ] **Baseline Capture:** Save a "Pre-Launch" version of the [Offer-Change Log](./growth-audit-offer-change-log.md) to track delta.

---

## 2. Post-Publishing (24h): Social Signal Extraction
*Run this 24 hours after publishing to extract signal from noise. Refer to the [Post-Publication Review Workflow](./post-publication-review-workflow.md).*

- [ ] **ICP Alignment:** Review the "Analytics" (LinkedIn/X). Are viewers/engagers Technical Founders, SMEs, or Operators?
- [ ] **High-Signal Comments:** Identify replies that ask "How?" or mention a specific business bottleneck.
- [ ] **Hidden Buyer Identification:** Check the "Like" list for high-value ICPs who didn't comment.
- [ ] **Screenshot Hook Performance:** Screenshot the post's initial performance (impressions/engagement) for the "Engine Room" archive.
- [ ] **Initial Triage:** Categorize replies as Curious Founder, Skeptical Operator, Potential Buyer, or Peer/Validator.

---

## 3. Interaction Capture: DMs & Discovery Calls
*Capture these fields after every qualified interaction. Use the [Beta Conversation Tracker](./growth-audit-beta-conversation-tracker.md).*

- [ ] **Exact Buyer Language:** Capture 1-3 verbatim phrases the buyer used to describe their pain (e.g., "I'm on a content treadmill").
- [ ] **Objection Capture:** Log the *exact* wording of any skepticism regarding price, time, or AI-efficacy.
- [ ] **Price Friction Score:** Rate the buyer's reaction to the Audit price on a scale of 1-5 (1 = no friction, 5 = hard pass on price).
- [ ] **Value Signal:** Which part of the diagnostic (Loops, POV Extraction, AI Skill Design) created the most "lightbulb moments"?
- [ ] **Qualification Score:** Score the prospect 1-5 based on the [Target Account Rubric](./growth-audit-target-account-rubric.md).

## 4. Evidence Management Rules

### Naming Conventions
Maintain a clean audit trail by naming all evidence files/screenshots as follows:
- **Posts:** `YYYY-MM-DD_[Channel]_[Theme]_Post.png` (e.g., `2026-05-19_LI_SystemOverSlop_Post.png`)
- **Conversations:** `YYYY-MM-DD_[Channel]_[ICP-Type]_Signal.png` (e.g., `2026-05-20_DM_SaaSFounder_Signal.png`)
- **Artifacts:** `YYYY-MM-DD_Artifact_[Name].png`

### Privacy & Redaction
Trinity prioritizes "Proof Discipline" and buyer privacy.
- **Names:** Redact/Blur all real names and profile pictures in public-facing "build-in-public" posts.
- **Companies:** Redact specific company names unless they are publicly attributable (e.g., a public case study).
- **Internal Only:** The [Beta Conversation Tracker](./growth-audit-beta-conversation-tracker.md) is for internal lab use only. Never share raw screenshots of it.

### Confidence Labeling (The Truth Meter)
Assign a label to every piece of evidence before it influences an offer change:
1.  **Verified:** Confirmed by payment, signed agreement, or approved public attribution.
2.  **Observed:** Directly witnessed in a recorded call, email, or DM thread.
3.  **Anecdotal:** Recalled from a conversation or reported by a third party.
4.  **Simulated:** Fictional or sample data used for demonstration (must be labeled).
5.  **Hypothesis:** An assumption about buyer behavior being tested.

## 5. Downstream Logging: Where Does It Go?

| Captured Item | Primary Destination | Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Post Analytics / Likes** | [Proof Capture Log](./proof-capture-log.md) | Log "Observed" signal from distribution. |
| **Exact Buyer Phrasing** | [Offer-Change Log](./growth-audit-offer-change-log.md) | Influence core copy and headlines. |
| **Buyer Objections** | [Objection Bank](./growth-audit-objection-bank.md) | Sharpen response scripts and FAQ. |
| **Price / Qualification** | [Beta Conversation Tracker](./growth-audit-beta-conversation-tracker.md) | Weekly decision matrix input. |
| **New Proof Artifacts** | [Proof Artifact Index](./growth-audit-proof-artifact-index.md) | Inventory for future promotion. |

## 6. Friday "Operator" Review
*Final check before the week is closed.*

- [ ] **Rollup Complete:** Are all DMs/Calls from the week logged in the [Tracker](./growth-audit-beta-conversation-tracker.md)?
- [ ] **Matrix Applied:** Run the evidence through the [Beta Decision Dashboard](./growth-audit-beta-decision-dashboard.md) Matrix.
- [ ] **Offer Updated:** If a change was triggered, is the [Offer-Change Log](./growth-audit-offer-change-log.md) updated?
- [ ] **Next Test Set:** What is the specific "Hypothesis" for next week's distribution?
