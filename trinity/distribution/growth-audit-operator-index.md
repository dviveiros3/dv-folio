# Growth Audit: Operator Index & Action Map

## Mission
This is the command center for the AI Growth Audit paid beta. It reduces cognitive load by mapping every artifact to a specific Job-to-be-Done (JTBD) and providing clear "if you need to..." guidance for Daniel during the launch.

---

## 1. The Operating Flow
The Growth Audit follows a 7-step loop to turn expertise into signal and signal into a productized offer.

1.  **Publish:** Ship POV and Proof posts using the [Launch Post Pack](./growth-audit-launch-post-pack.md).
2.  **Triage:** Process replies and DMs using the [Post-Launch Triage Guide](./growth-audit-post-launch-triage-guide.md).
3.  **Capture:** Log exact buyer language and objections in the [Manual CRM Specification](./growth-audit-manual-crm-spec.md) (Tab 2: Conversation Tracker).
4.  **Qualify:** Run 15-minute diagnostic calls using the [Discovery Call Script](./growth-audit-discovery-call-script.md).
5.  **Onboard:** Kick off the 1-week diagnostic using the [Buyer Onboarding Checklist](../catalog/growth-kit/growth-audit-buyer-onboarding.md).
6.  **Deliver:** Perform the audit, run the [Delivery QA Checklist](../catalog/growth-kit/growth-audit-delivery-qa-checklist.md), then hand over the buyer-specific diagnostic.
7.  **Review:** Update the offer every Friday using the [Beta Decision Dashboard](./growth-audit-beta-decision-dashboard.md).

## 3. "If you need to..." Next-Action Guidance

- **If you need to reply to a skeptical comment:** Open the [Triage Guide](./growth-audit-post-launch-triage-guide.md) and check the "Non-Fit" or "Skeptical" templates.
- **If you need to handle a "How much?" question in DMs:** Send the [Offer Sheet](../catalog/growth-kit/growth-audit-paid-beta-offer-sheet.md) and ask a qualification question from the [Discovery Script](./growth-audit-discovery-call-script.md).
- **If you need to disqualify a "Magic Button" seeker:** Use the [No-Fit Nurture](../catalog/growth-kit/growth-audit-no-fit-nurture.md) communication templates.
- **If you need to prepare for a discovery call:** Review the [Target Account Rubric](./growth-audit-target-account-rubric.md) and the [Discovery Script](./growth-audit-discovery-call-script.md).
- **If you need to prove the audit isn't just prompts:** Send the [Deliverable Walkthrough](../catalog/growth-kit/examples/growth-audit-deliverable-walkthrough.md).
- **If you need to log a new objection:** Add the exact buyer language to the [Objection Bank](./growth-audit-objection-bank.md) and update the [Conversation Tracker](./growth-audit-beta-conversation-tracker.md).

## 4. Launch Week: First 3 Actions
1. **Ship Post 1:** Publish the [Flagship Announcement](./growth-audit-launch-post-pack.md#1-post-1-flagship-linkedin-announcement-the-diagnostic) at 08:30.
2. **Setup the CRM:** Initialize your sheet using the [Manual CRM Specification](./growth-audit-manual-crm-spec.md).
3. **Execute Noon Triage:** Process first-wave replies using the [Triage Guide](./growth-audit-post-launch-triage-guide.md).

---

## 5. Friday Review: First 3 Actions
1. **Audit the Tracker:** Sum up Total vs. Qualified conversations in the [Conversation Tracker](./growth-audit-beta-conversation-tracker.md).
2. **Identify the Bottleneck:** Which loop (POV, Proof, Conversations, Offer) leaked the most this week?
3. **Apply the Matrix:** Use the [Decision Dashboard](./growth-audit-beta-decision-dashboard.md#1-weekly-decision-matrix) to choose next week's action (Continue, Sharpen, Pause).

---

## 6. Mandatory Proof-Safety Reminders
*Do not ship or say anything that violates these rules:*

- **No Outcome Guarantees:** We sell diagnostics and roadmaps, not leads or revenue.
- **AI as Leverage:** AI scales the founder's judgment; it does not replace their voice.
- **Label Everything:** Use "Sample," "Simulated," or "Hypothesis" for any artifact that isn't a verified client result.
- **High Status:** We are investigators and operators. We do not chase; we diagnose.
