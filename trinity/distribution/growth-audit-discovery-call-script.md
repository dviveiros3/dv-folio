# AI Growth Audit: Discovery Call Script

## Mission
To transition inbound interest or DM signals into a high-conviction diagnostic engagement. The goal is to identify if the founder has the raw materials (Expertise and Proof) to benefit from the [Distribution OS](../catalog/growth-kit/distribution-os.md) and if they are a fit for the [Growth Audit Paid Beta](../distribution/growth-audit-paid-beta-test.md).

## Call Standards
- **Duration:** 15 Minutes (Strict).
- **Tone:** Direct, calm, high-status, diagnostic.
- **Role:** Daniel is the lead investigator; the buyer is the subject matter expert.
- **Proof-Safety:** No lead guarantees, no fake urgency, no invented outcomes.

---

## 1. Context & Tone Set (2 Minutes)
*Goal: Frame the call as a diagnostic, not a sales pitch.*

"Thanks for jumping on. I keep these to 15 minutes because they are diagnostic—we're looking for where your distribution engine is leaking. I’ve looked at your [LinkedIn/Website/Artifacts], but I want to hear how you see the bottleneck. If there’s a fit, I’ll tell you how the Paid Beta works. If not, I’ll point you to a resource that fits better. Sound good?"

---

## 2. The Diagnostic (8 Minutes)
*Goal: Evaluate the five loops of the Distribution OS. Select 1-2 questions per loop based on pre-call research.*

### POV Loop (Authority & Thesis)
- "What is the one thing your market consistently gets wrong that you have a strong opinion on?"
- "If I asked your ideal buyer what you stand for, would they have a clear answer or a generic one?"

### Proof Loop (Evidence & Artifacts)
- "Do you have a library of inspectable proof, such as teardowns, data, or customer wins, that we can turn into public artifacts, or is most of your expertise still trapped in your head?"
- "What's the most recent 'Proof Artifact' you published?"

### Conversation Loop (Signal & Intent)
- "When you publish content, does it trigger DMs and specific business questions, or just vanity likes and comments?"
- "Are you currently doing manual outbound, or waiting for the inbox to light up?"

### Offer Loop (Productized Entry Points)
- "Do you have a 'wedge' offer—a small, high-value way for someone to buy your brain before a full implementation?"
- "Is your current offer a 'yes/no' decision, or a ladder of 'yeses'?"

### Conversion Loop (Feedback & Objections)
- "What is the most common objection you hear from qualified buyers right now?"
- "Do you have a system for feeding those objections back into your public content, or do they just die in the DM?"

---

## 3. Qualification & Red Flags
*Refer to the [Target Account Qualification Rubric](./growth-audit-target-account-rubric.md) to decide on fit.*

**Red Flags (Disqualify):**
- **The "Magic Button" Seeker:** "I just want AI to send 1,000 emails and get me leads while I sleep."
- **Zero-Proof Identity:** No product, no depth, no history.
- **The Volume Addict:** Cares about "going viral" more than qualified signal.
- **Low Agency:** Expects Trinity to "take it all off my plate" without founder input.

---

## 4. The Paid Beta Pivot (3 Minutes)
*Goal: Explain the Audit offer without fake urgency.*

"Based on what you’ve said, you have the [Expertise/Proof], but your [Loop Name] is the primary leak. I’m currently running a **Paid Beta for the AI Growth Audit**.

It’s a 1-week diagnostic where we:
1. Audit your POV and Thesis.
2. Map your specific loop leaks.
3. Design your first 2 custom AI skills (e.g., Insight Extractor).
4. Build a 90-day operating roadmap.

It’s a manual-first engagement. I use the lab to build the blueprint, and you or your team execute. Pricing is `[BETA_PRICE_PLACEHOLDER]` until the beta terms are finalized, and the paid beta exists to collect high-signal feedback while hardening the system."

---

## 5. Objection Handling (Proof-Safe)
*Based on the [Objection Bank](./growth-audit-objection-bank.md).*

1.  **On AI Slop:** "We don't use AI to *generate* ideas; we use it to *extract* and *atomize* your specific insights. The 'taste' stays yours; the 'lifting' goes to the model."
2.  **On Outcome Guarantees:** "I can't guarantee leads because I don't control your market or your execution. I can guarantee an engineering-grade diagnostic of your system and a roadmap to fix the leaks we've identified."
3.  **On 'Why a Paid Beta?':** "Trinity is a lab. We're hardening the Distribution OS. The Beta price reflects that we're looking for serious operators to test the diagnostic against real-world friction."
4.  **On Implementation:** "The Audit provides the blueprint. If you want the Lab to build it for you afterwards, we can scope a full implementation, but the Audit ensures we build the right system first."
5.  **On Time Commitment:** "I need 15 minutes for a voice note brief and 30 minutes for the roadmap review. The rest is on the Lab."

---

## 6. The Close Plan

### Outcome: YES (High Fit)
- "Great. I’ll send the payment instructions and the [Intake Checklist](../catalog/growth-kit/growth-audit-intake.md). Once the terms are confirmed and I have your Founder Tape, the 1-week clock starts. When can you get that Tape over?"

### Outcome: MAYBE (Skeptical/Needs Info)
- "Totally fair. I'll send you the [Sample Diagnostic Artifact](../catalog/growth-kit/examples/growth-audit-artifact-sample.md) and the [Deliverable Walkthrough](../catalog/growth-kit/examples/growth-audit-deliverable-walkthrough.md). Inspect the quality of the work. If it looks like the level of diagnostic you need, let me know."

### Outcome: NO (Not a Fit)
- "To be honest, I don't think the Audit is the right move for you right now because [Reason: e.g., you need to build more raw proof first]. I’d recommend following the [Lab Notes](../lab/README.md) for a few weeks to see how we think about [Specific Loop] before we look at a full diagnostic. Sound good?"
