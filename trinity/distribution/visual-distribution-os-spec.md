# Trinity Distribution OS: Visual Spec (v2)

This document is a production-ready design brief for the Distribution OS visual artifact. It follows the Trinity Proof Discipline and aligns with the Lab's engineering-first aesthetic.

## 1. Visual Concept: The Engineering Diagnostic
**Style:** "System Architecture" or "Circuit Diagram" aesthetic. High-density, professional, and precise.
**Visual Meta-Theme:** A closed-loop engine. Each stage feeds the next, with the final stage (Conversion) feeding back into the first (POV).
**Avoid:** Generic funnels, "growth" arrows pointing up-and-to-the-right, stock illustrations of people, or "shiny" SaaS gradients.

## 2. Layout Specifications

### LinkedIn Square (1:1)
*   **Dimensions:** 1080px x 1080px
*   **Composition:** Centralized pentagon or circular loop. High focus on legibility for mobile scrolls.
*   **Safe Zone:** Keep all critical text within the central 900px square.

### X Wide (16:9)
*   **Dimensions:** 1600px x 900px
*   **Composition:** Horizontal left-to-right flow with a return loop from right back to left.
*   **Safe Zone:** Avoid the bottom-right corner (X often overlays duration/expand icons).

## 4. Design Tokens (Grounded in Storefront)

### Typography
*   **Primary (Headings/Labels):** Inter (Bold/Semi-bold, Uppercase, Tracking: 0.1em).
*   **Secondary (Values/Metadata):** JetBrains Mono (Regular, Monospace).
*   **Vibe:** Technical documentation, not marketing copy.

### Color Palette
*   **Background:** `#FAFAFA` (Off-white) or `#0F172A` (Slate-950 for dark mode).
*   **Primary Elements:** `#0F172A` (Slate-900).
*   **Secondary Text/Lines:** `#64748B` (Slate-500).
*   **Accents (Status/Signal):**
    *   Success/Active: `#16A34A` (Green-600)
    *   Warning/Diagnostic: `#B45309` (Amber-700)
*   **Borders:** Slate-200 or Slate-800.

## 5. Accessibility & Readability
*   **Contrast:** Minimum 4.5:1 ratio for all text (WCAG AA).
*   **Minimum Font Size:** 24px for taglines on the 1080px canvas.
*   **Line Weight:** Use consistent pixel widths for connecting lines (e.g., 2pt or 4pt) to maintain the architectural feel.

## 6. Hardened Proof-Safety Rules
*   **NO Fictional Metrics:** Do not include fake follower counts, revenue numbers ($1M+), or conversion percentages (25% conversion).
*   **NO Fake Logos:** Do not use logos of unverified clients or partners.
*   **MANDATORY Labeling:** The graphic must include a small "LAB EXHIBIT" or "SIMULATED FRAMEWORK" label to distinguish it from a retrospective case study.
*   **VERB Discipline:** Use "Designed to," "Framework for," or "Operating System" in any captions.
