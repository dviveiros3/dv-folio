# Sales & Marketing Machine

## Inbound Engine
1. **Content Marketing**
   - Weekly blog posts targeting customer pain points
   - SEO-optimized content clusters
   - YouTube tutorials and walkthroughs
   - Free tools and calculators

2. **Email Marketing**
   - Weekly newsletter with valuable insights
   - Drip sequences for trial users
   - Re-engagement campaigns
   - Partner co-marketing emails

3. **Social Media**
   - LinkedIn thought leadership (daily)
   - Twitter/X product updates (weekly)
   - YouTube video content (bi-weekly)
   - Community engagement (daily, 15 min)

4. **SEO**
   - Targeted keywords per product
   - Content clustering strategy
   - Technical SEO audit
   - Backlink building

## Outbound Engine
1. **Cold Outreach**
   - Targeted prospect lists
   - Personalized email sequences
   - LinkedIn outreach
   - Follow-up cadences

2. **Partnerships**
   - Integration partners
   - Affiliate programs
   - Co-marketing agreements
   - Channel partners

3. **Events**
   - Virtual workshops
   - Webinars with customers
   - Industry conferences
   - Local meetups

## Conversion Optimization
1. **Landing Pages**
   - A/B test headlines and CTAs
   - Social proof placement
   - Pricing page optimization
   - Exit intent offers

2. **Free Trial Optimization**
   - Shorten time to value
   - Guide users to activation
   - Send milestone congratulations
   - Trigger upgrade prompts

3. **Retention**
   - Onboarding sequences
   - Feature adoption campaigns
   - Win-back offers
   - Annual prepay discounts
