# Email Sage — Product Specification

## Overview
A personalized cold email automation platform with AI-powered personalization and A/B testing.

## Problem
- Cold email sucks. 90% of outreach is generic and ignored.
- Personalizing each email manually takes forever.
- A/B testing is neglected because it's complicated.

## Solution
Email Sage writes personalized cold emails based on recipient research, tests variations, and optimizes send times.

## Target Customer
- Sales development reps (SDRs)
- Freelance consultants
- Startup founders doing outreach
- Marketing teams with outbound motions

## Key Features
1. Recipient Research AI
2. Personalization Engine
3. Multi-variant Testing
4. Smart Scheduling
5. Inbox Rotation
6. Reply Classification
7. Follow-up Sequences
8. Analytics Dashboard
9. CRM Integrations
10. Compliance Checker

## MVP Scope (Phase 1)
- Single mailbox (Gmail/G Suite)
- Basic recipient research (LinkedIn)
- 5-email sequences
- 3 A/B tests per campaign
- Basic analytics
- CSV import
- Stripe billing

## Pricing
- Free: 50 emails/month
- Pro: $49/month
- Growth: $199/month
- Enterprise: $499/month

## Success Metrics
- Free to paid: > 15%
- Reply rate improvement: 2x vs non-personalized
- Monthly churn: < 5%
