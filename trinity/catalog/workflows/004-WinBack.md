# Workflow: WinBack
## Customer Re-Engagement Campaign

### Description
Automated multi-channel campaign to win back churned or inactive customers with personalized offers and insights.

### Tools Used
- Make.com / Zapier
- HubSpot / ActiveCampaign
- Google Ads / Facebook retargeting
- Apollo / Hunter for alternative contacts
- GPT-4 for email copy
- Slack for alerts

### Trigger
Customer cancels or becomes inactive (30 days no login)

### Steps
1. Day 0: Send "We noticed you left" email
2. Day 3: SMS (if opted in) with special offer
3. Day 7: LinkedIn connection + personal note
4. Day 14: "What's changed since you left?" survey
5. Day 21: Case study of successful customer similar to them
6. Day 30: Last chance offer (30% off for 3 months)

### Error Handling
- Unsubscribe → attribute in CRM, stop outreach
- Re-engagement → log and celebrate
- Negative sentiment → route to support

### Deliverables
1. Win-back email sequences (5 templates)
2. Retargeting ad creatives
3. Survey questions
4. Offer strategy and pricing guidelines
5. Pricing: $99 (template) or $149/mo (managed)
