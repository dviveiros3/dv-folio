# AI Growth Audit: Buyer Intake Checklist

## Purpose
This checklist ensures we have the necessary raw material to perform a high-conviction diagnostic of your Distribution OS. We don't want generic information; we want the specific "truth" of your business.

## 1. Founder Context (Voice & Vision)
- [ ] **Founder Brief:** 15-minute recording (voice note or video) covering:
    - What is the one thing you believe that your market still gets wrong? (Your POV)
    - Who is the specific human that pays you, and what is their most expensive problem?
    - If you couldn't use "AI" or "transformation" to describe your value, what would you say?
- [ ] **Proof Inventory:** Links to 3-5 assets that have historically "worked" (e.g., a post with high-quality replies, a successful sales deck, a customer testimonial).

## 2. Distribution Loop Data
- [ ] **POV Loop:** Your 3 most common "soapbox" topics.
- [ ] **Proof Loop:** A list of case studies, screenshots of results, or data points we can use as "proof artifacts."
- [ ] **Conversation Loop:**
    - Where do your best leads currently come from?
    - 5-10 screenshots of recent DMs or email replies from prospects (PII redacted).
- [ ] **Offer Loop:**
    - Current price points and "what they get."
    - The most common objection you hear on sales calls.

## 3. Technical & Operational Access
- [ ] **Content History:** Export of your last 30 days of LinkedIn/X content (or links to profile).
- [ ] **Outbound Baseline:** Samples of current outbound scripts or DM openers.
- [ ] **Tool Stack:** List of current tools for CRM, scheduling, and content (e.g., HubSpot, Calendly, Taplio, etc.).

## 4. Redaction & Privacy
- [ ] **PII Check:** Ensure all customer names, private emails, and sensitive financial figures are redacted or rounded before submission.
- [ ] **Confidentiality:** Confirm if any provided assets are strictly under NDA and cannot be referenced in simulated examples.
