# AI Growth Audit: Paid Beta Intake

## Purpose
This intake ensures we have the necessary "raw truth" to perform a high-conviction diagnostic of your Distribution OS. This is a paid beta engagement designed for founders with existing expertise and proof who want to operationalize their distribution using AI.

## 1. Paid Beta Readiness
*The Growth Audit is a high-speed diagnostic. Please confirm you are ready for the pace.*

- [ ] **Expectation Check:** I understand the Growth Audit is a 1-week diagnostic and roadmap, not a guarantee of leads, revenue, or "overnight" automation.
- [ ] **Engagement Check:** I can commit 60 minutes to this intake and 30 minutes for the final roadmap review call.
- [ ] **Implementation Check:** I have the internal or external capacity to act on the roadmap recommendations over the next 90 days.

## 2. Proof Inventory (The Raw Material)
*Trinity builds on truth. Label your existing assets using the Trinity Proof Categories so we know what we can safely "weaponize" in your content.*

- **Verified:** Results confirmed by source evidence (for example: analytics exports, CRM reports, signed customer references, or approved screenshots).
- **Observed:** Patterns seen internally but not yet externally validated.
- **Anecdotal:** Qualitative feedback, testimonials, or "one-off" wins.
- **Simulated:** Examples used for demonstration using fictional data.
- **Hypothesis:** Assumptions we intend to test in the market.

- [ ] **Inventory List:** Provide links or files for 3-5 assets, labeled with their current proof level.

## 3. Distribution Loop Self-Assessment
*Rate your current loops from 1 (Non-existent) to 5 (Systematized & Repeatable).*

- **POV Loop:** Do you have a clear, contrarian thesis that the market associates with you? [ ]
- **Proof Loop:** Do you have a library of inspectable artifacts that prove your POV? [ ]
- **Conversation Loop:** Does your content naturally trigger qualified DMs or replies? [ ]
- **Offer Loop:** Do you have a productized entry offer that solves a specific pain? [ ]
- **Conversion Loop:** Do you systematically feed buyer objections back into your offer? [ ]

## 4. High-Signal Buyer Inputs
*We need the specific "taste" of your business. Focus on raw signals, not polished summaries.*

- [ ] **The Founder Tape:** A 10-15 minute raw voice note or video covering:
    - **The Soapbox:** What is the one thing your market gets wrong?
    - **The Expensive Problem:** What is the most expensive pain your buyer has?
    - **The No-Buzzword Value:** Describe what you do without using "AI" or "transformation."
- [ ] **The Signal Log:** 5-10 screenshots of recent raw signals (DMs, email replies, LinkedIn comments). Redact PII but keep the buyer's exact phrasing.
- [ ] **Baseline Access:**
    - Links to your primary social profiles (LinkedIn/X).
    - 2-3 samples of current outbound scripts or DM openers.
    - A brief list of your current tool stack (CRM, Content, Outreach).

## 5. Redaction & Privacy
- [ ] **PII Redaction:** Ensure all names, personal emails, and sensitive financial data are blurred or removed from screenshots.
- [ ] **Confidentiality:** Explicitly mark any assets that are under NDA. Trinity will use these for internal diagnostic purposes only and will never feature them in public lab notes.

## 6. Fit Assessment
*The AI Growth Audit is a specialized tool. It is not for everyone.*

**A Good Fit If:**
- You have deep domain expertise but "random" distribution.
- You have existing proof but don't know how to package it for AI leverage.
- You want an operational system, not just more "content volume."

**Not A Fit If:**
- You are looking for a lead-gen agency to "take it off your plate."
- You have no existing proof or market thesis to build upon.
- You require guaranteed ROI or revenue outcomes from a 1-week diagnostic.
