# Growth Audit: Buyer Intake Checklist

This checklist ensures that the AI Growth Audit has the high-conviction context required to produce an actionable roadmap.

## 1. Founder Context (Provided by Buyer)
To ensure the "Point of View" audit is accurate, we require:
- [ ] **Market Thesis:** A brief summary of your core belief about how your market is changing.
- [ ] **Ideal Buyer Pain:** Top 3-5 specific problems your customers are currently facing.
- [ ] **Technical Taste:** Any specific constraints or non-negotiables regarding your brand voice or product positioning.

## 2. Asset Access (Provided by Buyer; Source Verification Required)
We audit your existing "Proof" signals. Please provide:
- [ ] **Proof Inventory:** Links to case studies, customer results, or technical teardowns. *Note: Trinity will flag any claims that require additional source verification for public use.*
- [ ] **Content Samples:** 3–5 examples of your best-performing LinkedIn posts, articles, or newsletters.
- [ ] **Sales Decks:** Your current primary sales presentation or product overview.

## 3. Tech Stack Review
To design your "AI Skill" architecture, we need to know:
- [ ] **Current CRM:** (e.g., HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive).
- [ ] **Distribution Channels:** (e.g., LinkedIn, X, Substack, Beehiiv).
- [ ] **Data Sources:** Where your lead data currently lives (e.g., Apollo, Clay, Manual CSVs).

## 4. Engagement Targets (Planning Assumptions)
What are your primary goals for the next 90 days? *Note: These are used as planning assumptions for the roadmap, not guaranteed outcomes.*
- [ ] **Primary Channel Focus:** Which channel needs the most immediate operational leverage?
- [ ] **Resource Availability:** Who will be responsible for reviewing and approving AI-generated drafts?

---
**Proof-Safe Disclaimer:**
All inputs provided in this checklist are treated as "Provided by Buyer." Trinity's role is to audit these inputs and design systems to operationalize them. We do not verify the truth of buyer-provided metrics unless explicitly engaged for a verification audit.
