# Workflow: Weekly Content Engine

## Objective
Convert a founder's weekly insights into a consistent, multi-channel content presence with minimal founder time (max 15 mins/week).

## Specification

### 1. Trigger
- **Time-based:** Every Friday at 2:00 PM.
- **Action-based:** Founder uploads a voice note or transcript to a shared folder.

### 2. Inputs
- **Primary:** Raw voice note (5-10 mins) or meeting transcript.
- **Secondary:** Current ICP Definition and Positioning Strategy documents.
- **Asset Library:** Brand voice guidelines and previous top-performing posts.

### 3. Steps
1.  **Transcription:** (If audio) Auto-transcribe using Whisper or similar.
2.  **Insight Extraction:** Use `Skill: Content Ideation` to find the "Gold Nuggets."
3.  **Drafting:** Generate 3x LinkedIn posts, 5x X (Twitter) posts, and 1x Weekly Newsletter draft.
4.  **Review Loop:** Move drafts to a "Review" column in Notion/Trello.
5.  **Human QA:** Founder or Marketing Lead reviews and tweaks (approx. 5-10 mins).
6.  **Scheduling:** Once approved, push to Buffer/Loomly/Typefully.

### 4. Outputs
- 3x LinkedIn Posts (formatted with hooks and CTAs).
- 5x X Posts (ready for thread or individual scheduling).
- 1x Newsletter draft.

### 5. QA Checks
- [ ] Does the hook grab attention in < 2 seconds?
- [ ] Is the tone consistent with the founder's brand?
- [ ] Does every post have a clear "Next Step" or CTA?
- [ ] Is the content formatted correctly for the platform (e.g., no hashtags on LinkedIn)?

### 6. Maintenance Cadence
- **Monthly:** Review "Analytics Top Performers" and feed them back into the `Skill: Content Ideation` to refine the engine.
- **Quarterly:** Update "Brand Voice" guidelines based on evolving founder perspective.
