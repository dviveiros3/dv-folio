# Compliance and Ethics: Trinity Growth Kit

While AI provides immense leverage, it also requires strict adherence to privacy laws, platform terms, and ethical standards. Use this guide to ensure your growth engine remains compliant and reputable.

## 1. Outbound & Privacy Regulations
When using the **Outbound Prospecting Engine**, you must comply with regional regulations (e.g., CAN-SPAM in the US, GDPR in the EU, CASL in Canada).

- **Consent:** Ensure you have a legal basis for contacting prospects. For B2B in many regions, "Legitimate Interest" may apply, but verify with legal counsel.
- **Unsubscribe:** Every outbound email must include a clear, functional way to opt-out.
- **PII Handling:** Do not feed sensitive Personally Identifiable Information (PII) into public AI models. Anonymize data where possible (e.g., "Prospect at Company X" instead of full names/emails) unless using enterprise-grade LLM deployments with data privacy guarantees.

## 2. Platform Terms of Service
- **LinkedIn:** Avoid aggressive automated scraping or messaging that violates LinkedIn’s User Agreement. We recommend using official APIs or manual exports from Sales Navigator.
- **AI Providers:** Ensure your use of AI-generated content complies with the terms of your LLM provider (e.g., Anthropic, OpenAI). Most prohibit using AI to generate deceptive spam.

## 3. Scraping Cautions
- Respect `robots.txt` files when using tools to scrape prospect websites for personalization data.
- Avoid "deep scraping" of personal social media profiles; stick to professional/public-facing business data.

## 4. Ethical AI Usage & Transparency
- **Human-in-the-Loop (HITL):** AI should never send an email or publish a post without human review. This is your primary defense against hallucinations and brand-damaging errors.
- **Deception:** Do not use AI to impersonate a human in a deceptive way. The goal of the Growth Kit is to scale the *reach* of your authentic expertise, not to create "fake" personas.

## 5. Reputation Management
- **Domain Health:** Monitor your email sending domain reputation. High bounce rates or spam reports from poorly targeted AI outbound can "burn" your domain.
- **Brand Voice:** Ensure the AI doesn't produce content that is out of character or offensive. Regular audits of the "Context Foundation" are required.
