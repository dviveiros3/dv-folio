# Buyer Setup Checklist: Trinity Growth Kit

Before deploying the Growth Kit, ensure you have the following inputs, accounts, and roles ready. This ensures the AI has the "fuel" it needs to generate high-leverage outputs.

## 1. Required Inputs & Assets
- [ ] **Founder Voice Samples:** 3–5 examples of "gold standard" content (LinkedIn posts, emails, or articles) written by the founder.
- [ ] **Product/Service Overview:** Documentation of what you sell, how it works, and the core problems it solves.
- [ ] **Existing ICP Data:** Any existing customer profiles, case studies, or notes on "ideal" vs. "nightmare" clients.
- [ ] **Objections List:** A list of the most common reasons prospects say "no" or "not now."

## 2. Technical Infrastructure
- [ ] **LLM Access:** An account with a high-reasoning model provider (e.g., Anthropic Claude Pro, OpenAI ChatGPT Plus, or API access).
- [ ] **Lead Data Source:** Access to a prospecting tool (e.g., Apollo.io, LinkedIn Sales Navigator, or Clay).
- [ ] **Content Repository:** A place to store and track AI drafts (e.g., Notion, Trello, or a simple Google Drive folder).
- [ ] **(Optional) Automation Platform:** If scaling beyond manual use, an account with Make.com, Zapier, or n8n.

## 3. Human Roles & Review
- [ ] **The Strategist (Founder/Head of Growth):** Responsible for the initial "Context Foundation" and final quality sign-off.
- [ ] **The Operator (Junior Marketer/VA):** Responsible for running the workflows, handling initial AI outputs, and moving tasks through the pipeline.
- [ ] **Compliance Officer:** Responsible for reviewing outbound lists against "Do Not Contact" lists and ensuring privacy standards are met.

## 4. Success Metrics (KPIs)
Define what "success" looks like for this implementation. Suggested metrics:
- **Production Volume:** Number of high-quality content drafts produced per week.
- **Lead Quality Score:** % of AI-scored leads that meet the "High Intent" threshold.
- **Engagement Rate:** Reply rates on AI-drafted outbound vs. previous manual benchmarks.
- **Time Reclaimed:** Hours per week the founder moves from "drafting" to "reviewing."
