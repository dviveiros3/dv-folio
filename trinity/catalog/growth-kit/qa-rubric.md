# QA Rubric: "Ready to Publish/Send"

Before any output from the Trinity Growth Kit is sent to a prospect or published to a platform, it must pass this 5-point rubric. The Operator performs the first pass; the Strategist provides final approval.

## 1. Accuracy & Truthfulness
- [ ] Does the output contain any "hallucinations" (false claims about our product, the prospect, or the industry)?
- [ ] Are the names, company names, and titles 100% correct?
- [ ] If data is cited, is it verified?

## 2. Brand Voice & Tone
- [ ] Does this sound like the founder? (Compare against "Gold Standard" samples).
- [ ] Is it free of "AI-isms" (e.g., "In the ever-evolving landscape," "delve," "tapestry")?
- [ ] Is the tone appropriate for the specific prospect or audience?

## 3. Value & Relevance
- [ ] For content: Does this provide a unique insight or just state the obvious?
- [ ] For outbound: Is the personalization meaningful, or does it feel like a "mail merge" trick?
- [ ] Does it address a specific pain point identified in our ICP definition?

## 4. Compliance & Safety
- [ ] Does it include the required unsubscribe mechanism (for emails)?
- [ ] Does it avoid making "guaranteed" claims that could lead to legal liability?
- [ ] Has it been checked against the "Do Not Contact" list?

## 5. Formatting & Technical Polish
- [ ] Are there any awkward formatting artifacts from the LLM (e.g., "[Insert Name Here]")?
- [ ] Are all links functional and tracked correctly?
- [ ] Is the length appropriate for the medium (e.g., LinkedIn vs. Cold Email)?

---

**Pass/Fail:** If any checkbox is not marked, the output must be returned for revision or manually corrected before moving to "Ready."
