# AI Growth Audit: Delivery QA Checklist

This checklist defines the quality bar for the AI Growth Audit diagnostic before it is delivered to a buyer. It ensures "Proof-Safety," technical specificity, and commercial readiness for the [Paid Beta](./growth-audit-paid-beta-offer-sheet.md).

---

## 1. Proof-Safety & Claims Check
*Ensuring Trinity never makes unearned promises or fake traction claims.*

- [ ] **No Outcome Guarantees:** Does the audit avoid promising specific lead counts, revenue, or conversion rates?
- [ ] **Claim Classification:** Are all claims labeled correctly according to [Proof Discipline](../../brand/proof-discipline.md) (Verified, Observed, Anecdotal, Hypothesis)?
- [ ] **Truthfulness:** Are all citations of the buyer's results or market data 100% verified against their inputs?
- [ ] **Language Polish:** Is the diagnostic free of hype (e.g., "10x," "revolutionary," "automated wealth")?

## 2. Specificity & "Gold Nugget" Check
*Ensuring the audit isn't generic AI consulting.*

- [ ] **Founder Point of View:** Does the audit identify at least one contrarian or unique thesis ("Gold Nugget") found in the Founder Tape?
- [ ] **Loop Identification:** Is the primary bottleneck in the [Distribution OS](./distribution-os.md) clearly identified and supported by data/observation?
- [ ] **No Generic Slop:** Are the recommendations specific to the buyer's niche, avoiding "one-size-fits-all" marketing advice?

## 3. Buyer-Input Integrity
*Verifying the audit is grounded in the provided raw material.*

- [ ] **Founder Tape Integration:** Does the diagnostic quote or reference specific language from the 15-minute voice/video brief?
- [ ] **Proof Inventory Usage:** Are the buyer's provided case studies or results used as the foundation for the content/outbound strategy?
- [ ] **Signal Log Alignment:** Does the "Conversation Loop" diagnostic address the actual buyer language and objections found in the Signal Log?

## 4. AI Workflow Architecture
*Ensuring the technical blueprint is buildable.*

- [ ] **Tool Realism:** Are the recommended AI skills (e.g., Insight Extractor) architecturally sound and based on existing capabilities?
- [ ] **Manual-First Logic:** Does the workflow design prioritize human judgment/taste over pure automation?
- [ ] **Clear Triggers/Outputs:** Does each AI skill design include a defined input (trigger) and a usable output?

## 5. Roadmap & Actionability
*Ensuring the 90-day sequence is realistic.*

- [ ] **Prioritized sequence:** Does the roadmap start with fixing the highest-leverage bottleneck first?
- [ ] **Resourcing:** Is the implementation path (Self-Service, Kit, or Custom) clearly defined and scoped?
- [ ] **Weekly Milestones:** Does the 90-day plan break down into inspectable weekly tasks?

## 6. Privacy & Redaction
*Protecting buyer data and Trinity's operating secrets.*

- [ ] **PII Redaction:** Have all personal emails, phone numbers, or sensitive financial data been redacted from the final diagnostic?
- [ ] **Third-Party Data:** Are there no mentions of other clients' sensitive data in this report?
- [ ] **Internal Notes:** Have all internal drafting notes or "raw" LLM prompts been removed from the final artifact?

## 7. Review-Call Readiness
*Preparing for the 30-minute Roadmap Review.*

- [ ] **Executive Summary:** Is there a 1-page summary that the buyer can read in 5 minutes?
- [ ] **Top 3 Questions:** Have you identified the 3 most important questions the buyer needs to answer during the call?
- [ ] **Next Steps:** Is the "Post-Audit Implementation" section customized to the buyer's specific constraints?

---

## Disallowed Claims List (The "Never" List)
*If any of these are present, the audit fails QA.*

- [ ] "This will guarantee [X] leads/month."
- [ ] "Our AI system eliminates the need for founder judgment."
- [ ] "We have already achieved this result for [Company Name]" (Unless verified and approved).
- [ ] "This is a 100% automated growth machine."
- [ ] Any use of "game-changing," "tapestry," or "delve."

---

## Decision Gate: Send / Hold / Revise

**Status:** `[PENDING / READY / REVISE]`

- **REVISE:** If any "Proof-Safety" or "Buyer-Input" boxes are unchecked.
- **HOLD:** If the "Founder Tape" was insufficient to produce high-signal output.
- **SEND:** All boxes checked, PII redacted, and roadmap is actionable.
