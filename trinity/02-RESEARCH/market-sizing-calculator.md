# Market Sizing Calculator

## TAM, SAM, SOM Framework

### Total Addressable Market (TAM)
Formula: Total number of target companies × average contract value

Example: Email Sage
- Total businesses doing cold outreach: 5M
- Average annual contract value: $1,200
- TAM = 5M × $1,200 = $6B

### Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM)
Formula: TAM × % that can be reached × % that need solution

Example: Email Sage
- TAM: $6B
- Addressable (English-speaking, digital): 40%
- Need solution: 30%
- SAM = $6B × 0.40 × 0.30 = $720M

### Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM)
Formula: SAM × realistic market share in 3 years

Example: Email Sage
- SAM: $720M
- Realistic share (Year 1): 0.01%
- SOM = $720M × 0.0001 = $72K
- Year 2: 0.1% = $720K
- Year 3: 0.5% = $3.6M
