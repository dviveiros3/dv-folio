# Willingness to Pay Research

## Methodology
1. **Van Westendorp Price Sensitivity**
   - At what price would it be too expensive?
   - At what price is it a bargain?
   - At what price would you start questioning quality?

2. **Conjoint Analysis**
   - Present bundles of features at different prices
   - Measure preference and trade-offs

3. **Gabor-Granger**
   - Show price, ask purchase intent
   - Vary price to find optimal point

4. **Market Experimentation**
   - A/B test prices in live market
   - Measure actual conversion

## Per Product, Track:
- Price point with highest conversion
- Price point with highest revenue
- Price elasticity (how much volume drops when price rises)
- Optimal price for each customer segment
