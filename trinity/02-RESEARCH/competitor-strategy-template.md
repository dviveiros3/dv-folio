# Competitor Strategy Template

## Per Competitor
1. **Overview**: What they do, who they serve
2. **Strengths**: What they excel at
3. **Weaknesses**: Where they fail
4. **Opportunities**: What they are ignoring
5. **Threats**: How they would react if they saw us
6. **Pricing Strategy**: How they price and package
7. **Marketing Strategy**: How they acquire customers
8. **Product Strategy**: What they build and why
9. **Partnership Strategy**: Who they work with
10. **Exit/Acquisition Potential**: Would they acquire us? Could we acquire them?

## Trinity Positioning
- How are we different?
- How are we better?
- How do we win?
