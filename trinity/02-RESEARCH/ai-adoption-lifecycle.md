# AI Adoption Lifecycle Map

## Stage 1: Awareness (0-6 months)
- Customers: Awareness of AI, but not sure how it applies
- Pain: FOMO, confusion, analysis paralysis
- Opportunity: Education, awareness content
- Products: Free tools, guides, assessments

## Stage 2: Exploration (6-12 months)
- Customers: Testing AI tools, looking for use cases
- Pain: Too many options, don't know what works
- Opportunity: Proof of concept, pilot programs
- Products: Low-cost entry tools, test environments

## Stage 3: Implementation (12-24 months)
- Customers: Integrating AI into workflows
- Pain: Integration challenges, change management
- Opportunity: Implementation services, training
- Products: Workflow tools, consulting, training

## Stage 4: Optimization (24+ months)
- Customers: Scaling AI across organization
- Pain: Managing multiple tools, cost, governance
- Opportunity: Platform solutions, enterprise features
- Products: Enterprise platforms, governance tools

## Stage 5: Transformation (36+ months)
- Customers: AI is core to business model
- Pain: Keeping competitive, innovation
- Opportunity: Innovation partnerships, custom AI
- Products: Custom AI development, strategic consulting
