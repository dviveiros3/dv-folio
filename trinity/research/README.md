# Trinity Research

Market validation, competitor analysis, pricing experiments, and intellectual property research to ensure we build what people will pay for.

## Research Priorities
1. **Market Sizing** — Total addressable market per product
2. **Competitor Mapping** — Feature, price, positioning analysis
3. **Customer Discovery** — Pain points, willingness to pay
4. **Pricing Research** — Optimal price points per segment
5. **IP Landscape** — Patent, trademark, and copyright research
