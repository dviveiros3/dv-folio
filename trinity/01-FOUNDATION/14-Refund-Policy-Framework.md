# Refund Policy Framework

## Standard Policy
**30-Day Money-Back Guarantee**

No questions asked. If the product doesn't meet expectations, full refund within 30 days of purchase. Period.

## Specifics
- Refund processed to original payment method
- 5-10 business days for bank processing
- Works once per customer per product
- Doesn't apply to services already delivered (consulting, custom work)
- Annual plans prorated if refunded
- Abuse policy: >2 refunds flagged for review

## No-Refund Items
- Custom or bespoke deliverables
- Consulting hours already used (prorated)
- Third-party pass-through costs
- Enterprise deals with signed contracts

## Process
1. Customer requests refund via email or support
2. Automated acknowledgment within 1 hour
3. Review within 24 hours (check usage patterns)
4. Approve if within policy, explain if denied
5. Process refund within 3 business days
6. Send feedback survey (optional)
7. Mark in CRM

## Rationale
- **Trust**: Removes purchase friction
- **Competitive**: Most AI tools have flaky refund policies
- **Quality**: Forces us to build products that work
- **Conversion**: Increases trial-to-paid conversion
- **Feedback**: Refund requests are product feedback
