# Trinity Product Taxonomy

## Classification System

### Tier 1: Product Category
```
├── Applications (SaaS)
│   ├── Web Apps
│   ├── Browser Extensions
│   ├── Mobile Apps
│   └── Desktop Apps
├── Workflows (Automations)
│   ├── Zapier/Pipedream Packs
│   ├── n8n/Make Scenarios
│   ├── Custom Scripts
│   └── API Integrations
├── Playbooks (Knowledge Products)
│   ├── Step-by-Step Guides
│   ├── Templates & Frameworks
│   ├── Video Courses
│   └── Certification Programs
└── Skills (Capabilities)
    ├── Prompt Libraries
    ├── Tool Integrations
    └── Agent Behaviors
```

### Tier 2: Business Vertical
- **Marketing** — Content, SEO, social, ads
- **Sales** — Prospecting, closing, retention
- **Operations** — Process, logistics, compliance
- **Finance** — Accounting, FP&A, investment
- **Product** — Design, development, QA
- **HR** — Recruiting, training, culture
- **Legal** — Contracts, IP, compliance
- **Customer Success** — Support, onboarding, retention

### Tier 3: Customer Segment
- **Solopreneurs** (1 person, <$100K revenue)
- **Small Business** (2-20 people, $100K-$5M)
- **Mid-Market** (21-200 people, $5M-$50M)
- **Enterprise** (200+ people, $50M+)

### Tier 4: Delivery Format
- **Self-Service** — Buy and use immediately
- **Done-For-You** — We implement for you
- **Hybrid** — Self-service with optional implementation
- **Licensing** — White-label for agencies/consultants

### Tier 5: Price Point
- **Freemium** — Free tier with paid upgrades
- **Entry** — $9-$49/month
- **Growth** — $49-$299/month
- **Pro** — $299-$999/month
- **Enterprise** — $999+/month (custom)

## Product Naming Convention

APP-MKT-SOL-001 = Application, Marketing, Solopreneur, #1
WFL-OPS-SMB-045 = Workflow, Operations, Small Business, #45
PLB-FIN-ENT-120 = Playbook, Finance, Enterprise, #120

## Product Status Tracking
- IDEA: Concept stage
- VALIDATE: Market validation in progress
- BUILD: In development
- ALPHA: Internal testing
- BETA: Customer testing
- LAUNCH: Live and selling
- SCALE: Proven, optimizing for growth
- SUNSET: Phasing out
