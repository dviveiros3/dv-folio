# IP Protection Strategy

## Trademarks
- **Trinity**: Wordmark and logo trademark filed
- **Product names**: Reserve before launch
- **Taglines**: "Stop building demos. Start shipping products."
- Monitor for infringement quarterly

## Copyright
- All content © Trinity, year
- Code: Licensed under proprietary terms
- Documentation: Copyrighted, freely available
- Creative: Licensed properly for commercial use

## Patents
- Evaluate patentable IP per product
- Document prior art and innovation
- Cost vs benefit analysis before filing

## Trade Secrets
- Prompt engineering techniques
- Proprietary algorithms and training data
- Customer lists and internal processes
- Non-disclosure for all contractors and employees

## Open Source Strategy
- Core framework may be open sourced for adoption
- Commercial extensions remain proprietary
- Dual licensing for enterprise
- Community edition vs professional edition

## Enforcement
- Regular IP audits
- Cease and desist for infringements
- Budget for legal action if needed
