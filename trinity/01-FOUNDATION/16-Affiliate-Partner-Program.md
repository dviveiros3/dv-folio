# Affiliate and Partner Program

## Affiliate Program (Referral)

### Commission Structure
- **Tier 1**: 20% recurring for first 12 months
- **Tier 2**: 25% after $10K in referred revenue
- **Tier 3**: 30% after $50K in referred revenue
- **Lifetime**: Commission for first 12 months of customer

### Affiliate Resources
- Unique referral link for tracking
- Banner ads and creative assets
- Email swipe copy
- Social media post templates
- Case studies and success stories
- Dedicated affiliate manager (email)

## Partner Program (Reseller/White-Label)

### Tiers
- **Silver**: 15% discount for clients, no minimum
- **Gold**: 25% discount, $1K/month minimum
- **Platinum**: 35% discount, $5K/month minimum, co-branded

### Requirements
- Signed partner agreement
- Completed partner training
- Reference customer
- Active website or established business

## Payment
- Net 30 for commissions
- PayPal or wire transfer
- Minimum payout: $50
- Monthly payment run on 15th of following month
