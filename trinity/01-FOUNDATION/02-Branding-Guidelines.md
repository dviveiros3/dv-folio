# Trinity Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity

### Name
**Trinity** — The atomic building block stack: workflows, playbooks, and skills that together form a complete sellable product.

### Tagline
"Stop building demos. Start shipping products."

### Mission
Build 1000 tested, sellable AI assets that people actually pay for.

### Vision
Become the go-to source for ready-to-deploy AI business infrastructure.

## Visual Identity

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: #0A0A0F (Near Black) — Authority, power
- **Secondary**: #FF3D00 (Neon Orange) — Energy, action, urgency
- **Accent**: #00E5FF (Cyan) — AI, technology, future
- **Success**: #00FF88 (Neon Green) — Growth, validation
- **Warning**: #FFD600 (Yellow) — Attention needed
- **Neutral**: #8A8A8A (Gray) — Supporting text

### Typography
- **Headlines**: Inter Bold (700), uppercase for impact
- **Body**: Inter Regular (400)
- **Code/Technical**: JetBrains Mono (400)
- **Accent**: Space Grotesk (500), for creative elements

## Voice and Tone

### Principles
1. **Direct** — No fluff, no filler
2. **Action-oriented** — Every sentence drives forward
3. **Confident** — We know what works
4. **Technical but accessible** — No dumbing down, no gatekeeping
5. **Result-focused** — Always back to the outcome

### Examples
| Don't Say | Do Say |
|-----------|--------|
| "This could potentially help you" | "This will get you results" |
| "We think this might work" | "This is tested and proven" |
| "Feel free to try" | "Execute this now" |
| "It is our belief that" | "Here's what works" |
| "We would love to" | "We're building" |

## Logo Usage
- Full logo: Trinity wordmark + trident icon
- Icon only: Trident mark for favicon/badge use
- Wordmark only: Trinity in brand font
- Minimum size: 24px icon, 48px wordmark
- Clearspace: Minimum 2x icon height on all sides

## File Naming for Assets
- `trinity-logo-[color]-[size].[ext]`
- Examples: `trinity-logo-full-512x512.png`, `trinity-icon-cyan.svg`

## Product Badge System
- **Alpha**: Yellow badge, unproven
- **Beta**: Orange badge, tested but not validated
- **Live**: Green badge, selling now
- **Proven**: Purple badge, validated revenue
