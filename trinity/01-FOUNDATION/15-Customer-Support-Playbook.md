# Customer Support Playbook

## Channels
- **Email**: support@trinity.ai (primary)
- **In-app chat**: Intercom or Crisp
- **Knowledge base**: Self-service articles
- **Community**: Private Slack or Discord
- **Video**: Loom for complex issues

## Response Times
- **Enterprise**: < 1 hour during business hours
- **Pro**: < 4 hours
- **Growth**: < 24 hours
- **Entry**: < 48 hours
- **All**: Auto-acknowledge within 15 minutes

## Support Categories
| Priority | Description | Response | Resolution |
|----------|-------------|----------|------------|
| P0 (Critical) | System down, data loss | 15 min | 4 hours |
| P1 (High) | Major feature broken | 1 hour | 24 hours |
| P2 (Medium) | Feature request, minor bug | 24 hours | 1 week |
| P3 (Low) | General question, feedback | 48 hours | 2 weeks |

## Tone Guide
- Empathetic but efficient
- Always assume positive intent
- If it's a bug: "You're right, that's on us. Here's how we fix it..."
- If it's user error: "Here's the easiest way to do that..."
- Never say "You should have known" or "It's obvious that"
- Follow up after resolution: "Is this working for you now?"

## Escalation Path
1. L1: Support agent resolves 80%
2. L2: Developer or specialist for technical issues
3. L3: Product/Engineering for bugs or feature requests
4. L4: Executive for escalations, complaints, or strategic accounts

## FAQ Knowledge Base
- Searchable by keyword
- Updated weekly based on support tickets
- Includes screenshots and Loom videos
- Categorized by product and topic
