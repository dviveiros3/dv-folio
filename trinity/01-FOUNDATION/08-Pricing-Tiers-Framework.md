# Pricing Tiers Framework

## Tier Structure

### Freemium
- **Price**: $0 (Free forever)
- **Users**: 1
- **Usage**: Limited to basic features
- **Support**: Community only
- **Purpose**: User acquisition, word of mouth
- **Conversion Target**: 10% to paid within 30 days

### Entry (Solo)
- **Price**: $29/month or $290/year
- **Users**: 1
- **Usage**: Core functionality, limited volume
- **Support**: Email, 48h response
- **Purpose**: Individual professionals, freelancers
- **Conversion Target**: 25% of free users

### Growth (Teams)
- **Price**: $99/month or $990/year
- **Users**: Up to 5
- **Usage**: Full functionality, moderate volume
- **Support**: Priority, 24h response, chat
- **Purpose**: Small teams, agencies
- **Conversion Target**: 40% of Entry users

### Pro (Business)
- **Price**: $299/month or $2,990/year
- **Users**: Up to 20
- **Usage**: Unlimited, premium features, API access
- **Support**: Dedicated account manager, 4h response
- **Purpose**: Mid-market companies
- **Conversion Target**: 30% of Growth users

### Enterprise
- **Price**: $999+/month (custom)
- **Users**: Unlimited
- **Usage**: Custom limits, dedicated infrastructure
- **Support**: White-glove, 1h response, SLAs
- **Purpose**: Large organizations
- **Conversion Target**: 10% of Pro users

## Pricing Philosophy
1. **Lead with value, not cost** — Price based on ROI
2. **Remove friction** — Annual billing = 2 months free
3. **Upsell naturally** — Features gated behind tiers
4. **Grandfather existing** — Never raise prices on existing customers
5. **Test aggressively** — A/B test pricing pages monthly

## Psychological Pricing
- Use 9-ending prices ($29, not $30)
- Anchor with Enterprise tier ($999) to make Pro ($299) feel affordable
- Highlight most popular tier (Growth, usually)
- Show monthly vs annual savings prominently
- Offer 14-day free trial, no credit card required

## Adjustment Triggers
- < 15% free-to-paid: Lower price or increase free value
- > 40% churn in first month: Onboarding problem or price too high
- > 25% downgrade: Feature misalignment in tiers
- > 50% on lowest tier: Value not clear for upgrades
