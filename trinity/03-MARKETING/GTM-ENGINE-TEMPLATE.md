## Conversion Funnel
### Top of Funnel
- Blog post with CTA to free trial
- YouTube video with link to landing page
- Social media post with link to free tool
- Paid ad to landing page

### Middle of Funnel
- Landing page → free trial signup
- Free tool result page → product signup
- Case study CTA → demo request
- Webinar sign-up → product trial

### Bottom of Funnel
- Free trial → upgrade to paid
- Demo → proposal → close
- Retargeting ad → purchase
- Email campaign → purchase

## Retention Strategy
1. **Onboarding**: Personal welcome, quick wins, check-ins
2. **Engagement**: Feature education, tips, community
3. **Expansion**: Usage-based upsells, annual discounts
4. **Win-back**: Cancellation flows, offer, 3-month discount
5. **Advocacy**: Referral program, case studies, testimonials
