# Email Sage Go-To-Market Playbook

## Pre-Launch (Week -4 to -1)
### Positioning
- USP: "Personalized cold emails in 10 minutes, not 10 hours"
- Key benefit: 3x reply rates without manual research
- Target hero: Sales Development Representative (SDR)
- Secondary: Growth marketer, startup founder, agency owner

### Assets
- Landing page (designed, Dev ready)
- Explainer video (60-90 seconds)
- 3 case studies (with metrics)
- 5 social proof posts
- Email sequence (welcome, trial, upgrade, win-back)
- Pricing page (clear, 4 tiers)
- FAQ (top 20 questions, all answered, searchable)

### Channels
- ProductHunt (launch with founder story)
- HackerNews (technical story, show code)
- Reddit (r/sales, r/startups, r/marketing)
- LinkedIn (founder posts, case studies)
- Twitter/X (thread on sales automation)
- YouTube (explainer and tutorial videos)
- Podcasts (sales, SaaS, growth)

### Pricing Strategy
- Free: 50 emails/month (acquisition)
- Pro: $49/month (profitability)
- Growth: $199/month (volume)
- Enterprise: $499/month (floor and consultative)

## Launch Day (Week 0)
- 00:00 UTC: Landing page live, Stripe live mode enabled
- 00:00 UTC: ProductHunt listing active

## Week 1 Goals
- 1,000 unique visitors
- 100 signups

- 3 paying customers

## Month 1 Goals
- 300 signups
- 60 free trials
- 15 paying customers
- $1,200 MRR
- < 5% churn

## Customer Acquisition Strategy
### Organic
1. SEO: Target keywords ("cold email automation", "personalized outreach", "AI sales emails")
2. Content: Weekly blog posts, tutorials, case studies
3. Community: Answer questions on Reddit, LinkedIn, IndieHackers
4. Referrals: 20% commission program

### Paid
1. Google Ads: Target "cold email tools", SMB budget ($1K/month)
2. LinkedIn Ads: Target SDR titles ($1K/month)
3. Twitter/X Ads: Engage sales community ($500/month)
4. Newsletter sponsorships ($100-200 each)
5. Retargeting: All visitors who didn't convert ($200/month)
Total monthly paid budget: $3,000

### Partnerships
1. Sales training companies (co-marketing)
2. CRM platforms (HubSpot, Pipedrive marketplace)
3. SaaS directories (G2, Capterra, ProductHunt)
4. Influencer marketers with sales reach (affiliate)
5. Co-working spaces and accelerators

## Retention Strategy
1. Week 1: Personal check-in from founder (email/envoy)
2. Week 2: Feature education based on usage (email)
3. Week 3: Community invite (Discord/Slack)
4. Week 4: Monthly usage report + tips
5. Ongoing: Monthly automations until cancelation
6. Annual discount: 2 months free (pay annually)
7. Win-back: 30% discount for 3 months after cancelation
