## Weekly Review
1. Check spend vs budget
2. Review CTR, CPC, CPA by ad
3. Check landing page conversion
4. Pause underperforming ads
5. Scale top performers
6. Update creative if CTR < 1%

## Monthly Optimization
1. Reallocate budget to best performing audiences
2. Refresh creative (every 2-4 weeks)
3. Test new ad formats
4. Review and update landing pages
5. Analyze customer quality by campaign
