## WHAT TO SAY IN DISCOVERY CALL

**Minute 1-5: Ask**
- "What's your current monthly ad spend?"
- "What's your current CAC? By channel?"
- "How do you attribute conversions?"

**Minute 5-15: Listen**
- Let them vent frustrations
- Take notes
- Nod, don't sell yet

**Minute 15-20: Diagnose**
- "So you're spending $X but don't know which half is working?"
- "If you could see exactly which campaigns are profitable, what would that be worth?"

**Minute 20-25: Prescribe**
- "I can fix this in 30 days"
- "I'll reduce your CAC by 30% or work free"
- "$10k setup, pays for itself in month 1"

**Minute 25-30: Close**
- "Should we start Monday?"
- Send contract on call
- Get deposit same day
